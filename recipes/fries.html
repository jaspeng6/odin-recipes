THE BEST EASY AIR FRYER FRENCH FRIES RECIPE

Description

French fries... the epitome of the perfect side dish with anything.

This recipe will allow you to save so much oil, while enjoying the fruits of your minimal labour because of the ease of the instructions.

Ingredients

  • 3 large russet potatoes
  • 2-3 tablespoons olive oil
  • Sea salt and pepper, to taste

Directions

  1. Using a mandoline, slice the potatoes into frie shapes, then place into a nice cool water bath for an hour.
  2. Preheat the air fryer to 375 degrees
  3. Drain the water, and marinate fries with a couple tablespoons of olive oil, salt and pepper.
  4. Add fries to air fryer basket and cook for 13 minutes. Flip at 6.5 minutes
  5. Enjoy your golden brown fries!
